The Stroy of Origin

In previous article, we discussed, how the water originated on Earth.

We know that water exist in three natural forms on earth as ice water and vapor. In fact, 71% of the Earth's surface is covered with water and the earth is the only planet on which water can exist in liquid form on the surface. Why is this? The answer lies both in the earth size and its position in the solar system. The earth is big enough so that its atmosphere exerts enough gravitational pull to hold in water vapor. At the same time, it is located 125 million kilometers from the sun. This keep sit near the center of narrow zone where the temperature allows water to exist in its three natural forms ice water and vapor in the case of our neighboring planets Mars is now a frigid desert and the surface temperature of Venus over 999 degrees F is too much high for ice or liquid water to exist there.
